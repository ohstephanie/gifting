TLDR

24-25 academic year - Junior spring. Super checked out!

This semester is great. My classes are on the chiller side, and I don't really have to code in either of my COS classes. The only coding I'm really doing is for VIS208, but it's the kind of coding that makes me happy. I really love making websites and thinking about coding as something fun, not in terms of like a COS assignment. It's relaxing to just turn my brain off (for the most part) and make something that's cool and looks nice. I'm also taking steel band with Irene, Matthew, Inwoo, and a couple other friends. This is like my dream schedule. The only bad parts are my IW and 445, but those are mandatory so oh well...This is my final hurrah and then I'll be chilling next year (hopefully)!

Because my courses are on the chiller side this semester, it's the first time I've been slacking off so hard. It's like my work ethic is disintegrating but also this is allowing me to be spontaneous and do things that I otherwise would never have done my first two years when I was locked in and uptight about time management. I've been dilly-dallying super hard and hanging out with friends and new people all the time, which has done wonders for me. I'm not nearly as academically stressed as in my first two years, but of course I've been stressed about other things instead (job searching). Also because my two COS classes have optional attendance for lecture and precept, I genuinely have not gone since like week 2. Umm hopefully that's alright.

I also honestly have no clue what is going on with my independent work, but my seminar is super chill and I love the topic (digital humanities). Professor Kernighan is so cute and wholesome--he played classical music for us on the first day of class, and always wears PWICS merch.
