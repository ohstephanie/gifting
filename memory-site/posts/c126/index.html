Junior year.

In my junior year, I live two doors down from my room from sophomore year. Everything is the same, except it just takes 5 more steps to get back to my room when I come home. Our common room decor looks almost identical as to last year, too. The only difference is that I noticed that the bathrooms have been more messy this year.
