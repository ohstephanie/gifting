TLDR

23-24 academic year -- Sophomore spring. Overall good!

This semester is not very memorable off the top of my head which I think is a good thing, since I only remember remarkably bad semesters very vividly. I have my transcript pulled up to look at what classes I took.

Okay I remember now I think I didn't take any super fun classes this semester so I don't remember it too well. My favorite class was COS 333, where I made a whole app (NutriTiger!) with my friends Jewel, Rishabh, Eric, and Oyu. Probably the project I'm the most proud of from Princeton. I was really surprised that this group agreed to work with me because they are on another level cracked, but I learned a lot. It's also what finally gave me motivation to get into learning frontend development and making websites. NutriTiger got published as a Tiger App too!

Hydrology is a class my brother recommended me to take for my SEL requirement. It was actually pretty chill/easy/cool. I never went to class. But I would roll up to the 9am friday precept like 20 minutes late. I got closer with Chris and Angelica through this class, who are actually CEE majors.

I remember I really had fun at lawnparties this semester though! I also got into BodyHype and went to Puerto Rico over spring break. Lots of good things happened this semester, but academically I wasn't very interested in my classes (other than 333!).

CEE 306 Hydrology: Water and Climate
COS 324 Introduction to Machine Learning
COS 333 Advanced Programming Techniques
EGR 277 Technology and Society
