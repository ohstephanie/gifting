TLDR

22-23 academic year -- Freshman spring. I was always sick.

This semester was okay. I just remember being really sick all the time, like every other week. I also got norovirus, and I genuinely thought I was going to die. It was also the first time it snowed at princeton, but I didn't get to see it. I remember getting sick right when it started snowing, then once I recovered the snow had melted and only left a few half-melted snowmen on poe field. This was a crazy time, I had to get a writing seminar extension and I think I had to miss one of the classes. I was actually bedridden.

Other than that I remember being really happy this semester. I think spring semester is always just more fun than fall. 226 was kind of a hard jump from 126 but I made it out alive. I think at this point I still didn't have a lot of COS friends, so I was struggling to find people to work with and sit in class with. I ended up having to partner with my brother, who was also taking 226 at the time.

COS 226 Algorithms and Data Structures
KOR 108 Intensive Korean II
POL 396 International Organizations
WRI 136 Writing Seminar
